\chapter*{Abstract}
\label{CHP:ABSTRACT}%%
\thispagestyle{empty}

\PARstartLetter{S}{Systems} \altc{Simple record management systems,
sales, payments and product delivery, can serve small businesses in the food business quickly, modern and low cost. Systems thus have a well distributed architecture that can serve a wide audience in several locations and can be run from several modern devices like in smartphones for example, not losing its performance with the increase of the number of users. This way allowing a low cost when making the purchase of the system}

\altc{This work aims to show the development of a system based on Software Architecture oriented to applications in Font-End, focusing on a sales manager, payments and deliveries of products from a Doceria. I also present the technologies, standards and models used during development. }
